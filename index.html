TRUANT FU

We love music!

Truant Fu is an indie band from the capital city, where unique musical personalities converge. Formed in the summer of 2021, they are known for their bold, distinctive, and creative sound that brings powerful musical experiences to their audience. Describing themselves, Truant Fu stated, “Our band strives to explore and experiment with fresh musical elements, hoping to bring catchy and exciting experiences to listeners.” Their music blends Post-Rock and Funk-Rock, creating explosive energy and a dynamic presence on stage. Truant Fu gained wider recognition through the release of songs such as: Rối ren, Runaway, Hoa đăng, Một thời.
